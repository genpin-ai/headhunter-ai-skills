# Evaluation And Licensing

This public preview is designed to help teams decide whether the Headhunter AI Skills suite is relevant before discussing a private evaluation.

## Best-Fit Users

The suite is most suitable for:

- executive search firms
- recruiting consulting teams
- RPO or specialist recruiting teams
- boutique teams with 5 to 50 consultants
- teams that already use prompts but need repeatable workflow, review, and knowledge retention

It is less suitable for teams that only want a generic resume parser, a cold-call robot, or a one-off writing prompt.

## Available Cooperation Paths

### 1. Public Preview

Free to view.

Includes public positioning, workflow map, sanitized examples, and boundary notes.

Does not include installable packages or commercial rights.

### 2. Private Evaluation

For a team that wants to test the workflow on sanitized internal scenarios.

Typical scope:

- 1 to 3 priority skills
- fictional or sanitized test cases
- consultant feedback review
- adoption and risk notes

### 3. Team License

For recurring use by a recruiting team.

Typical scope:

- licensed internal skill package
- workflow onboarding
- usage boundaries and privacy guidance
- update cadence and support agreement

### 4. Pilot Deployment

For teams that want the skills connected to a working recruiting delivery system.

Typical scope:

- business object setup: client, role, candidate, process
- selected skill profiles
- content export and confirmation gates
- audit, permissions, and review workflow

### 5. Custom Skill Or Private Deployment

For teams with specialized workflows, security requirements, or private deployment needs.

Typical scope:

- custom skill design
- private knowledge and data boundary review
- deployment support
- training and handoff

## Suggested First Conversation

Bring one real business scenario, but remove or mask sensitive information before sharing in public channels.

Useful starting points:

- a role that is hard to calibrate
- a candidate who is hard to judge or communicate with
- an interview or offer case that is stuck
- a team workflow where information keeps getting lost
