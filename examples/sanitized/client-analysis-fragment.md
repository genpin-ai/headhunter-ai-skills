# Client Analysis Fragment

Fictional scenario: `Northstar Robotics`, a growth-stage industrial automation company, is hiring a regional sales director.

## Executive View

Northstar Robotics appears attractive to candidates who want to move from single-product selling into solution-based enterprise selling. The recruiting pitch should not overstate platform maturity. The safer story is: industrial automation demand is real, but the company needs people who can tolerate process building and cross-functional ambiguity.

## Consultant Notes

- Talent story: growth, technical product depth, enterprise customer learning curve.
- Verification needs: sales target ownership, pricing authority, delivery support, post-sale implementation responsibility.
- Candidate risk: people from mature foreign enterprises may struggle if reporting line, resources, or incentive rules are still changing.

## Candidate-Facing Boundary

Do not say: "The company has mature systems."

Safer language: "The role may include building parts of the regional sales process, not only inheriting an existing playbook."
