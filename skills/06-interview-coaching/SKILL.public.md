# Interview Coaching / 面试辅导

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Help consultants prepare candidates for interviews with different interviewer types while preserving factual accuracy and consultant control.

## When To Use

- A candidate has an upcoming interview.
- The consultant knows the role and candidate background.
- The interview may involve HR, senior executives, direct managers, or cross-functional stakeholders.

## Typical Inputs

- job information
- candidate profile and relevant project evidence
- interview round and interviewer type
- known concerns, sensitive points, or client expectations

## Expected Outputs

- consultant internal coaching focus
- phone-brief language for the consultant
- candidate-readable short notes
- interviewer-specific preparation points
- risk reminders and safe answer boundaries

## Boundaries

- Do not teach candidates to fabricate experience, data, motivation, salary, or project ownership.
- Do not expose consultant-only judgments directly to the candidate.
- If role or candidate information is missing, ask for the missing material before generating a full package.

The full version may include interviewer-specific templates, structured handoff contracts, and consultant training variants under a private license.
