# Offer Negotiation / Offer 谈判

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Help consultants design an offer-stage strategy that protects trust, closing probability, and long-term fit.

## When To Use

- A candidate is close to offer stage.
- Salary, title, scope, start date, family factors, competing offers, or client flexibility may affect closing.
- A consultant needs candidate-safe and client-safe language.

## Typical Inputs

- candidate motivation and constraints
- client feedback and approval path
- current and expected compensation details
- competing offer or counter-offer context
- role urgency, business value, and market supply
- interview debrief and prior communication records

## Expected Outputs

- push, cautious push, pause, or no-go recommendation
- pre-close readiness check
- offer structure and trade design
- BATNA and power-balance view
- candidate scripts and client scripts
- risk lights and execution rhythm

## Boundaries

- Do not expose candidate private family or financial details directly to the client.
- Do not expose client budget ceiling or internal leverage to the candidate.
- Do not use fake urgency or pressure tactics.
- Do not provide legal or tax advice; flag issues that require qualified confirmation.
