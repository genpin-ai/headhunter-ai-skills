# Public Opinion Ledger / 舆情线索台账

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Collect public online signals about a company into an internal evidence ledger for client analysis, candidate communication, and role judgment.

## When To Use

- A consultant needs public signals before presenting a company to candidates.
- A company has visible employee, consumer, compliance, or reputation signals.
- A role may be affected by public perception, employee sentiment, or operational risk.

## Typical Inputs

- target company name and aliases
- time range
- visible public sources
- user-provided screenshots or notes, if sanitized and permitted

## Expected Outputs

- public signal ledger
- source type, date, and evidence confidence
- employee and candidate sentiment clues
- consumer, product, service, legal, and compliance signals
- unresolved gaps and restricted-source notes

## Boundaries

- Public complaints, anonymous comments, screenshots, and social posts are leads, not verified facts.
- Negative information must keep source context.
- This skill does not produce risk ratings, BD advice, or candidate scripts by default.

## Full Version

The full version may include stricter evidence contracts, structured export, and integration with client analysis workflows under a private license.
