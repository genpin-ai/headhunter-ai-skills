# Client Analysis / 客户分析

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Help recruiting consultants understand a target company before pitching, sourcing, candidate briefing, or client calibration.

## When To Use

- A new client or target company enters the workflow.
- A consultant needs a candidate-facing company brief.
- A team needs sourcing angles, attraction points, risk notes, or verification questions.
- A monthly client update is needed.

## Typical Inputs

- company name
- target role or role family
- city, seniority, hiring reason, and audience
- public sources and user-provided notes
- previous report or update notes when available

## Expected Outputs

- company snapshot and latest status
- candidate attraction story
- role and talent implications
- market and competitor context
- risk and verification notes
- consultant pitch angles

## Boundaries

- Modern company facts should be current and source-aware.
- Missing salary, hiring process, layoffs, financing, or employee sentiment must be marked as unknown or pending verification.
- Candidate-facing language should not expose internal sourcing tactics.
