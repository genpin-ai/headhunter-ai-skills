# Candidate Communication / 沟通话术

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Help consultants judge how to approach a candidate before writing outreach scripts, follow-up language, recovery messages, and stop boundaries.

## When To Use

- A consultant has a candidate profile and a potential role.
- Candidate fit is unclear and requires judgment before outreach.
- A junior consultant needs copy-ready but evidence-aware communication support.
- A past conversation needs recovery or follow-up.

## Typical Inputs

- candidate resume, notes, or chat history
- client and role context
- JD or role analysis
- consultant concerns or prior replies

## Expected Outputs

- candidate level judgment
- fit and mismatch risks
- first outreach angle
- candidate concern prediction
- opening script and follow-up scripts
- verification questions
- recovery and stop boundaries

## Boundaries

- Judge before writing scripts.
- Do not produce generic outreach when the fit is weak.
- Do not include private personal identifiers unless explicitly required and safe.
- Distinguish evidence, assumptions, and missing context.
