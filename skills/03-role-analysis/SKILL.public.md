# Role Analysis / 岗位分析

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Help consultants understand whether a role is clear enough to search for, what the real hiring need may be, and which questions should be calibrated with the client.

## When To Use

- A new JD arrives.
- A client describes a vague or conflicting hiring need.
- Search quality is poor because the role standard keeps changing.
- A consultant needs intake questions before starting delivery.

## Typical Inputs

- role title and seniority
- JD or hiring notes
- business objective or current pain point
- reporting line, team setup, success criteria, and compensation range when available

## Expected Outputs

- role type and likely business context
- real responsibilities versus surface JD language
- must-have and negotiable requirements
- contradictions or unclear expectations
- client calibration questions
- downstream handoff notes for sourcing, communication, or interview work

## Boundaries

- Keep the work centered on the role itself.
- Do not automatically expand into salary benchmarking, talent mapping, company public opinion, or candidate outreach unless separately requested.
- If the role objective and responsibilities are unclear, the output should stay in preliminary judgment and calibration mode.
