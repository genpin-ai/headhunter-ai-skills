# Process Follow-up / 流程跟进

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Turn scattered recruiting process information into a practical follow-up view: current stage, reminder nodes, questions, risks, missing information, next action, and reusable follow-up record.

## When To Use

- A candidate or client process has a new update.
- A consultant needs today's follow-up priorities.
- Interview, offer, resignation, onboarding, or guarantee-period nodes may be at risk.
- Multiple processes need a compact board view.

## Typical Inputs

- candidate, client, role, and consultant
- current stage and latest follow-up
- interview date, offer status, onboarding date, or resignation status
- known concerns and promised next steps

## Expected Outputs

- process snapshot
- suggested reminder nodes
- questions for candidate, client, and consultant self-check
- risk signals
- next actions
- copy-ready follow-up record

## Boundaries

- Suggest reminder nodes; do not create real automations unless explicitly requested.
- Use absolute dates when enough information is available.
- Preserve uncertainty and mark inferred or missing information.
- Keep wording compact unless the user asks for scripts.
