# Interview Debrief / 整体评估复盘

Public preview version: v0.1.

This is a public preview, not the complete internal skill.

## Purpose

Help consultants review an interview outcome, compare client and candidate feedback, and decide whether to continue, pause, fight for the candidate, or redirect the process.

## When To Use

- A candidate has completed an interview.
- Client feedback and candidate feedback need to be reconciled.
- The process is approaching offer stage but key information remains unclear.
- A consultant needs a decision-ready internal evaluation.

## Typical Inputs

- interview notes
- client feedback
- candidate feedback
- prior resume report, role analysis, client analysis, or communication notes
- process status and known constraints

## Expected Outputs

- evidence completeness audit
- bidirectional intent judgment
- fit, mismatch, and risk diagnosis
- pre-offer information calibration
- investigation list
- consultant next actions and wording boundaries

## Boundaries

- Fidelity is more important than completeness.
- Vague positive feedback should not automatically become a push recommendation.
- Candidate pass-through does not mean offer negotiation has started.
- Conflicting evidence should be shown instead of smoothed over.
